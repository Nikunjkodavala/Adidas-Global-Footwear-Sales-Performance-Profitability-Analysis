# 📊 Adidas Global Footwear Performance & Profitability Dashboard

## 🚀 Overview

This project presents an interactive **Power BI dashboard** analyzing Adidas global footwear sales from **2018 to 2026**.

The dashboard focuses on:

* Revenue and profitability trends
* Impact of discounts on profit
* Marketing spend efficiency (ROI)
* Regional and category-level performance

It is designed to simulate a **real-world business intelligence solution** for decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Analyze global sales performance across countries and regions
* Identify key drivers of revenue and profit
* Understand the impact of discounts on profitability
* Evaluate marketing efficiency using ROI
* Provide actionable business insights

---

## 📂 Dataset

The dataset is **synthetically generated** using Python to simulate realistic business scenarios.

### Key Features:

* 📅 Date range: 2018–2026
* 🌍 Countries: USA, India, Germany, UK, UAE, Brazil, China, Australia
* 🛍 Categories: Shoes, Apparel, Accessories
* 📦 Channels: Online, Retail, Wholesale

### Metrics Included:

* Units Sold
* Revenue
* Cost
* Profit
* Discount
* Marketing Spend
* ROI

## 📌 Key Insights

* 📈 Revenue shows steady growth with seasonal and yearly trends
* 💸 Higher discounts negatively impact profitability
* 🌐 Online channel is the dominant sales driver
* 🏆 UAE leads in both revenue and profit
* 📉 Profit drops observed during low-demand periods (e.g., 2020)
